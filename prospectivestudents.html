N.C.E Admissions

To become a student of Imo State College of Education, one has to meet the following General Entry Requirements;

Age Limit:

No person under the age of sixteen (16) years may be admitted to the College as a student

Admission Requirements

Imo State College of Education, Ihittu-Uboma requires all candidates applying for admission into any of its courses to possess five credit level passes which must include a credit pass in English Language and a Science subject in not more than two sittings.

This institution has been approved by Joint Admission and matriculation (JAMB), and has been allowed to admit up to 2000 students per session.
